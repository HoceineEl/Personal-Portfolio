## Mediano – Sports Academies Management SaaS

### Overview

Mediano is a multi-tenant SaaS platform designed for sports academies, providing comprehensive management tools for training, attendance, events, and sales.

### Key Features

- **Multi-Tenant Architecture** – Each academy has unique branding
- **Subscription Management** – Flexible billing per academy
- **Training Sessions** – Schedule and manage training
- **Attendance Tracking** – Monitor student attendance
- **Event Management** – Organize tournaments and events
- **Sales Management** – Track merchandise and fees
- **Secure Access** – Role-based permissions per academy

### Technologies Used

- **Laravel** – Backend framework
- **Livewire** – Real-time features
- **Alpine.js** – Frontend interactions
- **FilamentPHP** – Admin panels
- **Tailwind CSS** – Styling
