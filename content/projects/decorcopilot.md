## DecorCopilot – AI Interior Design SaaS

### Overview

DecorCopilot is an AI-powered interior design platform that transforms rooms using Google Gemini AI. Users can upload photos of their spaces and receive AI-generated design suggestions across 30+ styles.

### Key Features

- **AI Room Transformation** – Upload any room photo and get instant redesign suggestions
- **30+ Design Styles** – From minimalist to bohemian, industrial to coastal
- **Visual Search** – Find furniture and decor items that match your vision
- **Floor Planning** – Create and visualize room layouts
- **PWA Support** – Install as an app on any device
- **Dark/Light Mode** – Comfortable viewing experience

### Technologies Used

- **Laravel** – Backend framework
- **Livewire** – Real-time interactions
- **FilamentPHP** – Admin panel
- **Google Gemini** – AI image generation
- **Tailwind CSS** – Styling
- **Paddle** – Payment processing
