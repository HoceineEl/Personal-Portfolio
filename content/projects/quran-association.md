## Quran Association Management System

### Overview

A comprehensive management system for Abi Zaid Al Qayrawani Association, handling attendance, finances, and communications with integrated WhatsApp messaging.

### Key Features

- **Attendance Management** – Track student attendance
- **Financial Management** – Fees and expense tracking
- **Role-Based Access** – Teachers, admins, and staff
- **WhatsApp Integration** – Automated reminders
- **Parent Messaging** – Direct communication channel
- **Reports** – Attendance and financial reports

### Technologies Used

- **Laravel** – Backend framework
- **Livewire** – Real-time updates
- **FilamentPHP** – Admin panel
- **WhatsApp API** – Messaging integration
- **Tailwind CSS** – UI design

### Client

Built for Abi Zaid Al Qayrawani Association.
