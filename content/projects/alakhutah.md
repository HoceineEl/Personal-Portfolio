## Ala Khutah – Historical Hijra Journey Platform

### Overview

Ala Khutah is a jobs portal for the historical Hijra journey project in Saudi Arabia – a 470km path with 7 overnight stations spanning 6 months of cultural tourism.

### Key Features

- **Recruitment Portal** – Job applications and screening
- **Geographic Filtering** – Saudi regions (Mecca, Jeddah, Medina, Rabegh)
- **Competency Assessment** – Tourism and heritage expertise evaluation
- **Workforce Management** – Staff scheduling and assignments
- **Arabic-Optimized UI** – RTL support with Arabic typography
- **Partner Network** – Integration with tourism authorities

### Job Categories

- Reception and hospitality
- Data analysts
- Sales supervisors
- Event management
- Tour guides

### Technologies Used

- **Laravel** – Backend framework
- **Livewire** – Dynamic forms
- **FilamentPHP** – Admin panel
- **Tailwind CSS** – Arabic-friendly styling
