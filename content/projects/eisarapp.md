## EisarApp – Multi-Tenant Training SaaS

### Overview

EisarApp is a multi-tenant SaaS platform for managing training workflows and employee development with comprehensive progress tracking and certification management.

### Key Features

- **Multi-Tenant Architecture** – Separate training environments
- **Training Workflows** – Structured learning paths
- **Employee Development** – Track individual progress
- **Progress Monitoring** – Visual dashboards and reports
- **Certifications** – Issue and manage certificates
- **Compliance Management** – Track required trainings

### Technologies Used

- **Laravel** – Backend framework
- **Livewire** – Interactive features
- **FilamentPHP** – Admin dashboard
- **Tailwind CSS** – Modern UI
